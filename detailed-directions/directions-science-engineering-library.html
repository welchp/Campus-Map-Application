The Science & Engineering Library is located in the Science Hill area of the campus, just south of McLaughlin Drive.

By bus: Those arriving by Metro bus can use the Baskin Engineering bus stop.

Parking: Parking is available in the Core West Parking Structure, located at the intersection of McLaughlin and Heller Drives. Permit parking is available throughout the structure; designated parking spaces on the second floor are available for hourly parking using a pay station. Proceed out the second floor entrance and walk between the Physical Sciences Building and Sinsheimer Laboratories. Turn right and walk up the stairs. When you reach the red brick plaza, the Science & Engineering Library will be on your left.

There are several parking options for those with mobility impairments, all of which are located along the Earth & Marine Sciences Building service road. The closest spaces are located at the library loading dock. There are also several spaces just past the Interdisciplinary Sciences Building; use the ramped entrance to that building to access the elevator. The Science & Engineering library is on the second floor level.

Parking permits are required in most lots on weekdays between 7:00 a.m. and 8:30 p.m. Lot designations are subject to change; it is advisable to check the permit requirements posted at parking lot entrances when parking on campus.

One-day visitor permits may be purchased at the main entrance Kiosk, or at the TAPS Sales Office located in the H Barn at the base of campus. Parking meters are posted regarding hours of enforcement.
