From Route 1: Take Reservation Road east through Marina to the Imjin Road stop light, three miles from Highway 1. Turn left on Imjin Road. The center is on the right hand side (the yellow and red buildings).

From Blanco or Davis Roads: Turn right onto Reservation Road and proceed west toward Marina to the Imjin Road stoplight. Turn right on Imjin Road. The center is on the right hand side (the yellow and red buildings).
