The Lower West Field is used for graduation ceremonies, and is located east of Heller Drive. It is best accessed from the West Entrance of the campus.

By car: At the Main Entrance, proceed west on Empire Grade, then turn right on Heller (the West Entrance). Turn right at the first opportunity to park in the West Perimeter Parking Lot.

Parking: There are two accessible parking places at the east end of the lot, closest to the field. Please be aware that there is a significant slope between those two spaces and the field itself. If you or someone in your party needs closer access due to disability-related needs, please have the graduate contact his or her college activities office to arrange this ahead of time.

Parking permits are required in most lots on weekdays between 7:00 a.m. and 8:30 p.m. Lot designations are subject to change; it is advisable to check the permit requirements posted at parking lot entrances when parking on campus.

One-day visitor permits may be purchased at the main entrance Kiosk, or at the TAPS Sales Office located in the H Barn at the base of campus. Parking meters are posted regarding hours of enforcement.
