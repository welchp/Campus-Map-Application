The Baskin Engineering complex is located in the Science Hill area of the campus, near the intersection of McLaughlin and Heller Drives. It is best accessed from the West Entrance of the campus.

By bus: Those arriving by Metro bus or campus shuttle should use the Science Hill bus stop; the Baskin Engineering buildings are north (uphill) of the bus stop.

Parking: Parking is available at the Core West Parking Structure, located at the intersection of McLaughlin and Heller Drives. Permit parking for faculty and staff (“A” permit) and graduate students (“B” permit) is available throughout the structure. Designated parking spaces on the second floor are available for hourly parking. Directions for payment are located nearby at a pay station that accepts both cash and credit cards. Accessible parking spaces are located near the Communications Building (east of the Baskin Auditorium), and on the south side of the Baskin Engineering Building (near the exterior stairwell).

Parking permits are required in most lots on weekdays between 7:00 a.m. and 8:30 p.m. Lot designations are subject to change; it is advisable to check the permit requirements posted at parking lot entrances when parking on campus.

One-day visitor permits may be purchased at the main entrance Kiosk, or at the TAPS Sales Office located in the H Barn at the base of campus. Parking meters are posted regarding hours of enforcement.
