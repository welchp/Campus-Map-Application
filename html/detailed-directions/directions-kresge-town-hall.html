The Kresge Town Hall is located in the northwest corner of the campus.

By foot or bus: Those walking or arriving by Metro bus or campus shuttle can take the pedestrian bridge from the Kresge College bus stop on Heller Drive.

Parking: For those driving, there are three parking options, listed in order of proximity to the Kresge Town Hall: the parking lot directly behind the Town Hall, which has four metered parking spaces; the parking lot across from the main entrance to Kresge College; and the Core West Parking Structure, which is across a pedestrian bridge from Kresge College. During the week, “A,” “B,” or “C” permits are required in these lots. The parking structure has 19 Pay Station spaces available to guests and short-term parkers, for up to two hours. When parking in these spaces, payment is made with cash or credit card to the pay station located next to the elevator on the second level of the structure. Two accessible parking spaces are available behind the Kresge Town Hall, plus another two further south across from the main entrance to Kresge College. 

Parking permits are required in most lots on weekdays between 7:00 a.m. and 8:30 p.m. Lot designations are subject to change; it is advisable to check the permit requirements posted at parking lot entrances when parking on campus. 

One-day visitor permits may be purchased at the main entrance Kiosk, or at the TAPS Sales Office located in the H Barn at the base of campus. Parking meters are posted regarding hours of enforcement.
